The internet is for people, not profit.

Mozilla fights for a healthy internet through open-source technology, research, and activism.

Our Mission

Mozilla's mission is to ensure the internet is a global public resource, open and accessible to all. An internet that truly puts people first, where individuals can shape their own experience and are empowered, safe, and independent.

Privacy Policy

We collect minimal data. We never sell your data. You can opt out of analytics at any time. We are transparent about what we collect and why.

Open Source

Firefox and all our products are open source — anyone can review, audit, or contribute to the code. Transparency is a core principle of everything we do.
